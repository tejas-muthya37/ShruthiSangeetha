Bio

Anupama Rao, a powerful and energetic singer, is an outstanding exponent of Carnatic Music, the classical music system of Southern India. Her strong and vibrant voice, rich and fertile imagination and an excellent blend of tradition and modernity has helped her to win a permanent place in the hearts of music lovers. Her music has an endearing quality that connects her to any audience that she performs for.


Anupama has undergone an intensive training period of 13 years under great musical teachers. She began her music career by learning vocal under Vidushi Smt Kusuma Keshava Rao. She then continued classical vocal and Veena with, Veteran Vidushi Smt T Sharadha, Former Head of the Dept Music in Maharani’s College. She is presently pursuing Vidwath in Carnatic classical vocal and Senior in Veena. She learnt light music with Vidushi Smt Deepa Natraj and Vidushi Shri Narahari Dixith.
Anupama continued her quest for knowledge and gets guidance for Kavya sangeetha from Hindustani classical music exponent Shri Shankar Shanbhogue. It is notable that Anupama also qualified herself as MA in Economics and gave it all up to pursue her passion for Carnatic music.
